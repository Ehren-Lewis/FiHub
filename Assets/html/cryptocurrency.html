About Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency, also known as crypto, is a type of currency that comes exclusively in digital form. Unlike phyiscal currenies, there is no central regulation or authority to maintain or uphold them. Crypto's purpose is to be a medium of exchange through a computer network, as they are not typically an accepted form of payment in most transactions. Other's use this as an opportunity for investment.

Since the creation of crypto, there have been thousands of cryptocurrencies created and will only continue to grow as popularity increases. Currently, the most popular, and well known, crypto is Bitcoin.

Besides for investment opportunities, there are many reasons why crypto is used. Some of the most popular reasons include:

  • The digital form allows anyone from anywhere to send or recieve payments with crypto.
  • Inflation is a thing of the past with crypto, as most have a limited supply.
  • The cost for transactions is lower compared with physical curriencies.

Even though there are many benefits with crypto, it is not free of risks. The main risk with crypto is the possibility of the value decreasing, due unforseen changes in the market. However, this risk exists with other types of investment opporunities too.
