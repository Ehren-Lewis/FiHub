About Stocks

Before diving into what stocks are, there are a few terms FiHub deems benefical that you should know

Stocks, also known as equity, are a type of security that give stockholders a share of ownership of a company. A shareholder is considered an owner determined by the number of shares they own relative to the number of outstanding shares of the company. Stock exhanges are regulated by their own organizations under the U.S Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). Stock brokers and brokerage firms are regulated by the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA).

Here are some useful definitions to better understand what the principles of stocks:

  • Share: A single unit of ownership in a company
  • Stock: Representation of the holder's part ownership of a given or multiple companies
  • A shareholder is determined by their own stocks relative to the total number of oustanding shares of the company
